Swiss Rail Resources

  • SBB Mobile App: Download for real-time train schedules, mobile tickets, and platform information.
  • Swiss Travel Pass: Available online at swisstravelsystem.com or at major train stations.
  • Half-Fare Card: CHF 120 for one month, gives 50% discount on all standard tickets.
  • Jungfrau Travel Pass: 3-6 day pass for unlimited travel in the Jungfrau region.
  • Glacier Express: Book at glacierexpress.ch. Seat reservation mandatory (CHF 39).

Printable Travel Checklist

Before You Leave India

  • Check passport validity (6+ months from travel date)
  • Apply for Schengen visa (4-6 weeks before travel)
  • Book flights and hotels
  • Purchase travel insurance
  • Exchange currency (CHF or EUR)
  • Inform bank about international travel
  • Download offline maps and translation apps
  • Purchase Swiss Travel Pass (if applicable)
  • Pack appropriate clothing for the season
  • Print all travel documents (digital copies as backup)

During Your Stay

  • Validate train/tram tickets before boarding
  • Keep passport copy with you at all times
  • Drink tap water (safe and free)
  • Use free public Wi-Fi when available
  • Try Swiss chocolate and fondue
  • Take scenic train rides (Glacier Express, Golden Pass)
  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Shop at Migros/Coop for affordable food
  • Ask for Tax Free forms when shopping (CHF 300+)
  • Respect local customs and etiquette
