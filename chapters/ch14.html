Honeymoon Itinerary

Overview

Switzerland is one of the most romantic destinations in the world, making it a perfect choice for honeymooners. This 7-day luxury honeymoon itinerary combines scenic beauty, romantic experiences, and unforgettable memories.

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich - Romantic Evening

Morning: Arrive at Zurich Airport. Chauffeur-driven transfer to luxury hotel.

Afternoon: Check into a lakeside luxury hotel (e.g., Dolder Grand or Baur au Lac).

Evening: Romantic candlelight dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant. Evening stroll along Lake Zurich.

Accommodation: The Dolder Grand (5-star) - CHF 500-800 per night.

Day 2: Lucerne - Lake Cruise and Old Town

Morning: First-class train to Lucerne (45 min). Private walking tour of Chapel Bridge and Lion Monument.

Afternoon: Private boat cruise on Lake Lucerne with champagne.

Evening: Candlelit dinner at lakeside restaurant. Stay at a luxury hotel overlooking the lake.

Day 3: Mount Pilatus - Golden Round Trip

Morning: Golden Round Trip: boat, cogwheel train, and cable car to Pilatus Kulm.

Afternoon: Romantic picnic at the top with panoramic Alpine views.

Evening: Return to Lucerne. Couples spa treatment at hotel.

Day 4: Interlaken and Jungfraujoch

Morning: Scenic train to Interlaken. Check into a luxury hotel with mountain views.

Afternoon: Cogwheel train to Jungfraujoch. Ice Palace, Sphinx Observatory, and champagne at the Top of Europe.

Evening: Romantic dinner in Interlaken.

Day 5: Grindelwald First and Lauterbrunnen

Morning: Gondola to Grindelwald First. First Cliff Walk for couples photo.

Afternoon: Visit Lauterbrunnen Valley. Romantic walk through the valley of 72 waterfalls.

Evening: Fondue dinner at a traditional Swiss restaurant.

Day 6: Zermatt and the Matterhorn

Morning: Scenic train to Zermatt. Check into a luxury spa hotel.

Afternoon: Gornergrat Railway for Matterhorn views. Professional couple photography session.

Evening: Candlelit dinner at Chez Vrony with Matterhorn views.

Day 7: Departure

Morning: Helicopter tour over the Matterhorn (optional, CHF 300-500 per person).

Afternoon: Last-minute shopping for Swiss chocolates and souvenirs. Transfer to airport.

Honeymoon Budget Estimate

Luxury 7-day honeymoon: CHF 5,000-8,000 (₹4,75,000-7,60,000) per couple excluding flights. Includes 5-star accommodation, fine dining, scenic trains, and premium experiences.
