Essential Travel Tips

Currency Exchange

Exchange currency at banks or official exchange counters for the best rates. Avoid exchanging at airports or hotels where rates are less favorable. ATMs are widely available and offer good exchange rates. Notify your Indian bank before traveling to avoid card blocks.

ATM Usage

ATMs are available throughout Switzerland. Most accept Visa, Mastercard, and Maestro cards. Withdrawal fees vary by bank. Use ATMs attached to banks rather than standalone machines for security. Set a daily withdrawal limit before traveling.

Mobile SIM Cards

Indian mobile SIM cards work in Switzerland with international roaming, but data charges can be high. Options for staying connected:

  • Swisscom: Prepaid SIM with data packages from CHF 10. Available at airports and Swisscom stores.
  • Salt: Budget-friendly prepaid options with good coverage.
  • Sunrise: Competitive prepaid plans with data packages.
  • eSIM: Apps like Airalo offer digital eSIMs with data packages for Switzerland.

Internet Access

Free Wi-Fi is widely available in hotels, restaurants, cafes, and public spaces. Many Swiss cities offer free public Wi-Fi in city centers and train stations. The SBB network provides free Wi-Fi on most trains.

Local Laws to Know

  • Carry your passport or a copy at all times - police can ask for identification.
  • Jaywalking is illegal and can result in fines.
  • Public drinking is generally allowed but may be restricted in some areas.
  • Tobacco smoking is banned in enclosed public spaces.
  • Drug laws are strict - avoid any illegal substances.
  • Speed limits are strictly enforced with heavy fines.

Safety Advice

Safety Tips for Indian Travelers
  • Switzerland is very safe, but watch for pickpockets in busy tourist areas.
  • Keep your bag zipped and in front of you in crowded places.
  • Avoid displaying expensive jewelry or large amounts of cash.
  • Use hotel safes for passports and valuables.
  • Be cautious of common scams like fake charity collectors or over-friendly strangers.
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, move away.
